Deliverables / A2: POVs & HMWs

Assignment 2: Point of View & How Might We

Defining our design challenge and opportunity space

Overview

Building on our needfinding research, we crafted Point of View (POV) statements that synthesize our understanding of users, their needs, and the insights we gained. These POVs helped us frame the design space and generate "How Might We" questions to guide our ideation.

This POV statement became the foundation for our design direction.

How Might We Questions

From our POV statements, we generated these "How Might We" questions to open up our design space:

How might we help [user] to [action] so that [benefit]?

How might we enable [user] to [action] without [constraint]?

How might we make [activity] more [quality] for [user]?

How might we reduce [pain point] when [user] needs to [action]?

Primary HMW: How might we [your chosen HMW question]?

Our Design Direction
