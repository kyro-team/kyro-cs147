Poster Content

Our poster will include the following key sections:

Project Title & Team

Clear project name, compelling tagline, and team member names.

Problem Statement

Brief description of the user need and problem we're addressing.

Target Users

Who are our users and what challenges do they face?

Research Insights

Key findings from needfinding that informed our design.

Design Process

Visual timeline showing iteration from low-fi to high-fi.

Final Solution

Showcase of high-fidelity prototype screens and key features.

Testing & Results

User feedback and validation of our solution.

Impact & Next Steps

Value proposition and future development plans.

Design Guidelines

Our poster follows these design principles:

Clear Hierarchy: Large title, organized sections with clear headers

Visual Focus: Images and screenshots take priority over text

Readable from Distance: Large fonts (minimum 24pt for body text)

Brand Consistency: Uses our project colors and design system

White Space: Not cluttered, breathing room between sections
