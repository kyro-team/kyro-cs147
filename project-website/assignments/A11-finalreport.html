Key Learnings

User-Centered Approach

Starting with deep user research and continuously validating with real users led to insights we never would have discovered through assumptions alone.

Iteration is Essential

Each round of prototyping and testing revealed new insights. Our final design is significantly better because we embraced iteration.

Power of Feedback

Early and frequent feedback, even on rough prototypes, helped us fail fast and redirect efforts toward what truly matters to users.

Balancing Priorities

Learning to prioritize features based on user needs vs. technical feasibility vs. team capacity was a critical skill we developed.
